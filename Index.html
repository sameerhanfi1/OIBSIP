Wear EDITH

The E.D.I.T.H Glasses are a pair of glasses designed by Tony Stark in the Marvel Universe.
Originally created and developed in 1893 by Caleb Bradham
and introduced as El Glassico, it was renamed as Huf Shayn in 1898, and then shortened to Tihde in 1961.
